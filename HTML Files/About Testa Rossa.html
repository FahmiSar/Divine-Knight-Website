Background

The Vermillion Knight also known as a Crimson Demon with a thousand weapons. Found in the depths of the Imperial Capital where it was sealed until Duke Cayenne took the young Prince Cedric and forced him to resonate with the Divine Knight. Duke Cayenne had plans of using the Vermillion Knight's power in order to win the power feud during the Civil War. Unfortunately for him Crow and Rean took down the berserk Vermillion Knight using the powers of their Divine Knights thus freeing Cedric from the Duke's evil hands. However the Vermillion Knight was cursed long ago causing the now pilot of the Knight, Cedric, to bear the malice from the Knight causing his personality to change drastically. This led Chancellor Giliath Osborne to seek out the Prince for his group known as the Ironbloods.
