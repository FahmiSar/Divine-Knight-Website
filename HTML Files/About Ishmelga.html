Background

The Ebon Knight, the true antagonist of the Trails of Cold Steel games and orchestrator of every disaster that befell the Erebonian Empire. Ishmelga had sought to create a great disaster in the form of the Great Twilight in order to resurrect the Great One, a being that had been split into the seven Divine Knights. Ishemlga's current awakener is Giliath Osborne, biological father of Rean Schwarzer. On April 22, S.1192, a group of pro-war mercenaries murdered Osborne's wife and mortally wounded Osborne's son along with setting their house ablaze. Osborne, who wasn't home at the time due to his job in the military, rushed home to find his wife and child at death's door. His wife's dying wish was to ensure that Rean survived, to which Osborne had cried out pleaing anything and anyone to save his son. To which Ishmelga revealed itself and made a deal with Osborne in exchange to save his son. This deal would cause Osborne to become the awakener of the Ebon Knight and lead him to be used by Ishmelga and his goal for a world war. During the final rivalry, Valimar won against Ishmelga causing him to fuse with Rean and Valimar. Together they vanquished Ishmelga with the help of the Holy Beast of Earth's power
