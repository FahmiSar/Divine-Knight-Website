Ordine, The Azure Knight is one of the Seven Divine Knights that contains the power of the Great One. Ordine's final awakener is Crow Armbrust. Ordine, Valimar and Zector are considered to be the weakest of the Seven Divine Knights according to Black Alberich. Ordine possess a "Boost Mode" that increases it's power. Similar to Crow, Ordine welds a double saber.
