Background

Valimar, the Ashen Knight is one of the seven Divine Knights that contain the power of the Great one. Currently there exists two known awakeners of Valimar however there are more unmentioned awakeners. Valimar's first known awakener is Drichels Reise Arnor, known as the Lionhearted Emperor. Drichels is known for his victory in the war of the lions that lead to modern day Erebonia. Valimar's second known and last awakener is Rean Schwarzer. A student of Thors Military Academy and protagonist of the Trails Of Cold Steel games. Similar to Rean, Valimar welds an eastern style sword called a Tachi. During the events of Trails Of Cold Steel 4, Valimar welds The Sword Of The End.
